    About Us

    M/S VIJAY TECHNICAL SERVICES & CONSTRUCTION PVT. LTD. was established in the year 1989 as a private limited company. We feel pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the competent, qualified engineering company offering all the services related to technical installation at very reasonable rates. Our expertise in the area of new electrical installation, plumbing & fire fighting installation on turnkey basis, facility management services, building renovation, modernization and augmentation works including civil and interiors, up-gradation of existing electrical, plumbing, fire fighting, security system and HVAC works is well known in market. Quality and assurances of our services is our evident clientele. Our area of operation is mainly in Delhi and NCR.

    Our Mission

    We are committed to respond to our customer’s needs in every possible way – from new product offering, environmental responsibility and communications to final delivery. As we renew and re – engineer our capabilities, our focus on quality, reliability and innovation remains constant.

    Our Values

    We work in open environment that embraces change, new ideas, respect with full passion and integrity, for the individual and equal opportunity to succeed. We live our values to measure our success with the success of our customers, shareholders, employees, communities and people.

    Our Belief

    We believe in partnerships focused on performance, environmental protection and in employing practices that allay public fears and perceptions in the matter of pubic health. Our strong belief is in pre–planning and coordinating the results to attain ascertained goals on time and as per client’s satisfaction.

    Services

    The primary focus of our company is on:

    New Electrical Installation

    From point wiring to 11 KV sub-station including supply, installation testing and commissioning of HT Panel, Transformers, D.G, Sets, L.T Panels, Sub Distribution panels, Distribution boards, cabling, cable trays, race ways, rising mains, conduiting, wiring, earthing, lighting fixtures etc. Also we undertake job related to fire alarm system, CCTV system, Burglar alarm and Security System, Telephone exchange & EPABX Systems, lifts etc. on turnkey basis.

    New fire-fighting installation

    From pump room to sprinkler including supply, installation testing and commissioning of main fire hydrant / sprinkler pump sets, diesel engine operated main hydrant & Jockey pump sets, hydrant riser & distribution system up to the line hydrants, automatic sprinkler distribution system, pump room piping installation, water curtain distribution system & fire extinguishers, stainless steel water distribution piping system etc.

    New plumbing installation

    From pump room to taps in the toilets including supply, .R.O. Systems, Sewerage treatment plants ( STP & ETP) External & Internal C.I/uPVC sewerage & drainage piping system, Solar thermo siphon / pressurized heating Systems, Oil – fired / Electrical central heating system, Swimming Pool re-circulating water distribution cum filtration system, borewell & rain water harvesting system, installation of sanitary wares & plumbing faucets etc.

    Facilities Management

    Include a comprehensive management of all core and non – core services that go into the day–to–day running of an office/building space and residences. This involves providing preventive and predictive maintenance services ranging from operation of various technical systems like water supply, power and Air – Conditioning systems etc. We believe that facilities management is more than just creating a functional work place.

    Advanced Maintenance

    Our company provides advanced maintenance Services in a Preventive, Predictive and Proactive manner. Customized approach in these maintenance programs ensures Reduced breakdowns, Smooth running of equipment, Avoid premature equipment failures, Preserve useful life of equipment, Energy saving. Value additions offered by us include Improving indoor Air Quality(IAQ), Thermographic Analysis of Equipments, etc.

    Repairs and refurbishment

    The company has expertise in up-gradation & modernization of existing electrical installations with minimum load shut-down. Repairing and modernizing technical installations related to HVAC, Plumbing, Electricals, Computer data networking, Water Treatment & STP, etc. We provide complete energy audit of services - both for new as well as running facilities and ensure energy conservation throughout all procedures.
